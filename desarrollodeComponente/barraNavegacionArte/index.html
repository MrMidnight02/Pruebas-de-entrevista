Mission of Desmotos Arts & Crafts: At Desmotos Arts & Crafts, we are dedicated to being the premier destination for art enthusiasts, providing high-quality paints, brushes, and art supplies to inspire creativity and personal expression. We aim to offer exceptional customer service, expert advice, and a diverse range of products to support artists at every level. Through our commitment to fostering creativity and inclusivity, we strive to be more than just a store—we aim to be a hub of inspiration and support for our community's artistic endeavors.

Brushes

Hacksaw Brushes

Made of the highest quality oak, Hacksaw brushes are known for their weight and ability to hold paint in large amounts. Available in different sizes. Starting at $3.00 / brush.

Art Frames (assorted)
